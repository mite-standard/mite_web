MITE data standard/repository

The Minimum Information about a Tailoring Enzyme (MITE) data standard/repository stores information on tailoring enzymes - enzymes that modify immature natural products. Each MITE entry stores information on a single enzyme: their reaction(s), substrate specificities, and known substrate-product pairs. It further integrates with other databases, such as UniProt, NCBI Protein, Rhea, and MIBiG.
