What happens next?

After a quick screening by one of the MITE staff members, your submission will be converted in the MITE file format and posted as an Issue on the MITE Data GitHub webpage. We try to do this quickly, but it may take a few days until it appears online.

Next, one or more of our volunteer Reviewers will inspect your submission in detail, correct fixable errors, or flag any issues that cannot be resolved. You are invited to participate in this discussion.

Once review is completed and the submission is approved, it will be added to the newest pending release of the MITE Data repository. Once the release in published, your submission can be accessed on the MITE Webpage.
