Meet the Developers

MITE is being developed by the following people

Mitja M. Zdouc
Project Lead and Lead Developer

Mitja is a pharmaceutical scientist from Austria, interested in method development for natural product biosynthesis and discovery using genomics and metabolomics.

Adriano Rutz
Developer

Adriano is a pharmaceutical scientist from Switzerland, interested in method development for the characterization of biological matrices using mass spectrometry-based computational metabolomics.

Daniel Probst
Developer

Daniel is a Swiss computer scientist turned bioinformatician interested in applying machine learning to biological and chemical questions to develop methods that support lab and field scientists.
